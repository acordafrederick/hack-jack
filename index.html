THE GAME
Instructions

Click on the playing cards to know the value of each one. Your goal is to increase your "SCORE" by matching the value of the provided "NUMBER." Every matched number increases your "WINS," and exceeding the number value increases your "LOSSES." The number and the value of each card change randomly each game round.

The key to racking up wins is to remember the value of each card and to add them up correctly. If you're out of luck, the values of the cards won't add up to match the number. May the odds be with you. Play on!
