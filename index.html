Card Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four playing cards below. By clicking on each of them you will add a specific amount of points to your score. You win the game by matching your score to the number; you lose if your total score goes above it. The value of each card is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the cards' value change. Good luck!
